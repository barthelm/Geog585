Lab Assignment / Milestone 15

The map i created with Geoserver-based styled WMS layers contain the State of New Mexico Geographical Data. The data listed in the overlays is New Mexico Vegetation classifications, cities and towns, wilderness boundaries, counties, annual precipitation contours and May 12 active wildfires locations. Also, shown on the map is USGS imagery for the Southwestern United States.

When first opening the map click on the blue box to turn on overlays and if needed use the zoom tool for enhancing the resolution of the map. The main purpose of the map is to show trends in areas of active wild fires locations relative to wilderness areas and annual precipitation amounts. With the current droubt conditions in New Mexico wild fires have increased in wilderness areas as shown on the map specifically in lower annual precipitation regions.
